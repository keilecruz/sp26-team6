## Description
> GlowUp is a beauty and grooming service booking platform that connects customers with trusted professionals such as hairstylists, nail technicians, and makeup artists.  
> The app aims to simplify the process of finding reliable beauty services by allowing users to browse verified providers, view portfolios, book appointments, and leave reviews.  
> The goal of GlowUp is to make beauty service booking safe, convenient, and transparent for both customers and providers.

## App Functions

### Customer (user role)
1. **Create/modify customer profile** – Customers can sign up, log in, and edit their personal profile information including name, contact details, and preferences.
2. **View available services** – Customers can browse a list of available beauty services filtered by category, provider, location, and availability.
3. **Subscribe to available services** – Customers can book appointments by selecting a service, choosing an available time slot, and confirming the booking.
4. **Write reviews for subscribed services** – After completing a service, customers can leave ratings and written reviews for the provider.

### Provider (service provider role)
1. **Create/modify/remove provider profile** – Providers can create and update their professional profile, including experience, services offered, and portfolio images.
2. **Create services** – Providers can add new services, set prices, and define availability time slots.
3. **View customer statistics** – Providers can view basic statistics such as number of bookings and average ratings.
4. **Reply to reviews** – Providers can respond to customer reviews to maintain engagement and trust.

### SysAdmin (admin role)
1. **Manage user access** – Admins can enable, disable, or remove user accounts.
2. **Moderate services** – Admins can approve or remove services that violate platform rules.
3. **Moderate reviews** – Admins can remove inappropriate or abusive reviews.
4. **View usage statistics** – Admins can view platform-wide statistics such as total users and bookings.
